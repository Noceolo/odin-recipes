Kanelbulle recipe

A swedish kanelbulle and how to make them


Ingredients

  • Milk
  • Butter
  • Flour
  • Yeast
  • Sugar
  • CInnamon
  • Cardamom

Steps

  1. Heat the milk and butter
  2. Mix the dough
  3. First dough rise
  4. Mix the filling
  5. Roll out the dough
  6. Form the buns
  7. Second dough rise
  8. Brush and sprinkle
  9. Bake
